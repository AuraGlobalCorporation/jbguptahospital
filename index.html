About Us

Compassionate Care Is What We Provide

Founded by the visionary Late Dr. J.B Gupta, JB Gupta Hospital has been a cornerstone of quality healthcare in Bhiwani for over 20 years. Our mission has always been to provide accessible, comprehensive, and compassionate medical care to every patient who walks through our doors.

From advanced diagnostics to round-the-clock emergency services, our dedicated team of physicians and specialists work tirelessly to ensure the health and well-being of our community.

Key Facilities

Advanced Infrastructure for Your Care

Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art medical infrastructure to handle everything from routine check-ups to critical emergencies.

Advanced ICU (Level II)

Fully equipped Intensive Care Unit with continuous monitoring, ventilator support, and 24×7 intensivist coverage.

24×7 DM/MD Doctors

Round-the-clock availability of super-specialist (DM) and specialist (MD) doctors for immediate medical attention.

Dialysis Services

In-house dialysis facility with modern machines for patients requiring renal replacement therapy.

POCUS & 2D Echo (24×7)

Point-of-care ultrasound and 2D Echocardiography available round the clock for rapid cardiac assessment.

Ventilator & Advanced Monitoring

High-end ventilators, multi-parameter monitors, and hemodynamic monitoring for critical patients.

Bedside Bronchoscopy

Diagnostic and therapeutic bronchoscopy available at the bedside for respiratory emergencies.

Oxygen & Emergency Care

Centralized oxygen supply with fully equipped emergency department operating 24/7.

Fully Computerized Lab

State-of-the-art pathology laboratory with computerized reporting and quick turnaround times.

ECG, X-Ray, ECHO, TMT

Complete range of diagnostic investigations including ECG, digital X-Ray, 2D Echo, and Treadmill Testing.
