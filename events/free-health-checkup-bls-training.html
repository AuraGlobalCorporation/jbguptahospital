About This Event

JB Gupta Hospital, Bhiwani is proud to announce a Free Health Check-up Camp combined with Basic Life Support (BLS) Training for the general public. This community initiative aims to promote preventive healthcare and equip citizens with essential life-saving skills.

Cardiac arrest and medical emergencies can happen anywhere — at home, in the workplace, or on the street. Knowing how to respond in the first few critical minutes can be the difference between life and death. This event will teach participants hands-on CPR, choking management, and emergency first aid — skills that can save a life.

The health check-up component includes free screening for blood pressure, blood sugar, BMI assessment, and a general consultation with our experienced doctors. Early detection of conditions like hypertension and diabetes can prevent serious complications down the line.

Free Health Check-up Includes

Blood Pressure (BP) Screening
Blood Sugar (Random) Testing
BMI & Weight Assessment
General Physical Examination
Heart Rate & Pulse Oximetry
Basic Eye Check-up
Respiratory Assessment

Recognizing Cardiac Arrest & Emergencies
Hands-only CPR Technique (Adults & Children)
Choking Management (Heimlich Maneuver)
Recovery Position & Airway Management
When & How to Call for Emergency Help
AED (Automated External Defibrillator) Awareness
First Aid for Common Injuries
Practical Hands-on Practice with Mannequins

Why BLS Training Matters

Sudden Cardiac Arrest kills thousands in India every year — many could be saved with immediate CPR.

For every minute without CPR, the chance of survival decreases by 7–10%.

Bystander CPR can double or triple the chances of survival.

BLS skills are not just for healthcare workers — every citizen should know them.

This training follows international guidelines and is conducted by certified professionals.
