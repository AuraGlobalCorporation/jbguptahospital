About Dr. Pawan Goyal

Dr. Pawan Goyal is a distinguished visiting endocrinology consultant at JB Gupta Hospital with elite qualifications from two of India's premier medical institutions — AIIMS New Delhi and SGPGIMS Lucknow. He specialises in the management of Type 1 & Type 2 Diabetes, Thyroid disorders, PCOD, hormonal imbalances, osteoporosis, and growth-related conditions in children. He provides expert, evidence-based care for complex metabolic and hormonal conditions. Dr. Goyal is available on the 1st and 3rd Sunday of every month from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM for OPD consultations.
