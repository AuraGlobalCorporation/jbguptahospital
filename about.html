Who We Are

Compassionate Care Is What We Provide

The JB Gupta's Hospital is consistently serving people of Bhiwani from last 20 years. The Hospital provides comprehensive, cost effective, patient centric treatment with the promise and commitment to safety with the best healthcare professionals.

Founded by the visionary Late Dr. J.B Gupta (MBBS, MD Medicine), the hospital has remained true to its motto: “More Than Care... Commitment”.
