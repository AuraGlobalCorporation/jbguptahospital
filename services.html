What We Offer

Our Healthcare Services

From diagnostic testing to emergency transport, we provide a complete spectrum of medical services under one roof.

2D Echo

2D Echo transforms sound into visual patterns for enhanced audio clarity and immersive experiences.

Spirometry

A vital test measuring lung function to diagnose and monitor respiratory health.

TMT - Treadmill Testing

A cardiac stress test to assess heart function during physical exertion.

X-Ray

A quick, non-invasive imaging technique to visualize bones and internal organs.

ECG

A quick, non-invasive test that records the electrical activity of the heart.

Laboratory

Comprehensive testing services for accurate health diagnosis and monitoring.

Blood Transfusion Services

A medical procedure where donated blood is transferred to a patient to replace lost blood or treat medical conditions.

Pharmacy

Offering expert medication management, consultations, and safe prescription services to support your health and well-being.

Ambulance Services

Providing quick and reliable emergency medical transport to ensure timely care during critical situations.
