About Our CCU

Welcome to the Critical Care Unit

Welcome to the Critical Care Unit at JB Gupta Hospital, Bhiwani. Founded by Dr. J.B. Gupta, the hospital has provided advanced medical services for over 20 years. The CCU focuses on severe or life-threatening conditions with comprehensive medical attention.

Our Critical Care Unit is equipped with the latest medical technology and staffed by a team of dedicated specialists who are committed to providing the highest level of intensive care to every patient, around the clock.

Services Offered

Comprehensive CCU Services

From round-the-clock monitoring to holistic patient support, our Critical Care Unit offers a complete range of services to ensure the best outcomes.

24/7 Intensivist Coverage

Round-the-clock availability of trained intensivists ensuring continuous expert supervision and rapid response to any changes in patient condition.

Continuous Patient Monitoring

Advanced multi-parameter monitoring systems track vital signs in real-time, enabling early detection and swift intervention for any complications.

Family Counseling & Support

Dedicated counseling sessions keep families informed about patient progress, treatment plans, and provide emotional support during difficult times.

Social Work & Spiritual Care

Compassionate social workers and spiritual care coordinators address emotional, social, and spiritual needs of patients and their families.

Physical Therapy Guidance

Early mobilization programs and guided physical therapy to aid recovery, prevent complications, and help patients regain strength and mobility.

Nutrition Support

Personalized nutrition plans crafted by clinical dietitians to meet the unique metabolic demands of critically ill patients and support healing.

Mental Health Resources

Psychological support and mental health services for both patients and families, addressing ICU-related anxiety, stress, and post-critical care recovery.
